About Our Clinic

Know Who's Treating You
Before You Book

Kimura is run by Ricky Arora, RMT. His background in weight lifting, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, and computer science shapes how he treats: practical, focused, and based on how your body actually moves.

Ricky works with a lot of tight backs, necks, shoulders, hips, and gym-related tension. Sessions often include deep tissue massage, trigger point work, sports massage techniques, and focused work on the areas that keep locking up or feeling overworked.

You will also get simple stretch or mobility suggestions when they make sense. Nothing complicated. Just practical things you can actually use between sessions to keep your neck, back, shoulders, hips, or legs moving better.

If you are nervous about pressure, pain, or not knowing what to ask for, that is normal. You can speak up at any point. Pressure, pace, focus areas, trigger point work, and cupping are always your call.
